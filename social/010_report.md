## Key Rules Reinforced This Batch

- Never position electro swing as "jazz but better" — jazz fans are the target market
- Never imply any part of the night is weak ("songs get better as it goes on")
- Never imply 24/7 operation ("the mic doesn't sleep")
- Thursday should not be positioned as quieter or lesser than Fri/Sat
- Friday/Saturday Stories need high-energy visuals — crowd alive, not empty room
- Song-listing for disco karaoke isn't working — need a new angle entirely
